Welcome to our Support Page

Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website?
This is a website meant to handle multiple maintainence requests for current residents of the HRDC;s buildings.
How do I submit a maintainence request?
In the bar on the left side of the screen, there is a button labeled "Dashboard". Clicking on this will take you to an main page of the site. Then, click on the symbol of a piece of paper with a plus sign in it. This will take you to the maintainence form. Your first and last names will be automatically filled, so you can then enter your phone number, address, unit, your request, building name, and whether or not Personal Conducting Maintainence may enter your building in your absense. After clicking Submit, you should receive an email confirming your information.
What is the information on the main page?
The main page shows a dashboard with your information. For a regular user without any special permissions, you'll be able to see a list of your own housing applications and a list of your own maintainence requests.
I found an issue with the site. How can I contact you and report it?
If you think you found a bug, glitch, issue, or think something about the site needs to be changed, feel free to go to the issues page on the site's Github here to open an issue. We'll try our best to address it as soon as possible!
I'm still confused and need a little bit more help.
That's okay, we have more detailed info on our user documentation page. The link to it can be found here. More information, as well as more detailed descriptions of the information here can be found there.
