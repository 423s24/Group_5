Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website?
This is a website meant to handle multiple functions for housing with HRDC. These mainly include submitting an application for housing and submitting maintainence requests for your current lodging.
How do I submit a housing application?
In the bar across the top of the screen, there is a link labeled "Housing". Clicking on this will take you to an application form. Your first and last names will be automatically filled, so you can then enter your desired unit and phone number to apply. After clicking Submit, you should receive an email confirming your information.
How do I submit a maintainence request?
In the bar across the top of the screen, there is a link labeled "Maintainence". Clicking on this will take you to an maintainence request form. Your first and last names will be automatically filled, so you can then enter your address, unit, your request, phone number, building name, and whether or not Personal Conducting Maintainence may enter your building in your absense. After clicking Submit, you should receive an email confirming your information.
What is the information on the main page?
The main page shows a dashboard with your information. For a regular user without any special permissions, you'll be able to see a list of your own housing applications and a list of your own maintainence requests.
I found an issue with the site. How can I contact you and report it?
If you think you found a bug, glitch, issue, or think something about the site needs to be changed, feel free to go to the issues page on the site's Github here to open an issue. We'll try our best to address it as soon as possible!
